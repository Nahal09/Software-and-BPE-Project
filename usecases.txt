# Restaurant Management System

## Overview

This Restaurant Management System is a comprehensive solution for managing the entire process of a restaurant, from taking customer orders to designing the menu, preparing food, and finally delivering it. The system is designed to streamline restaurant operations and enhance customer experience.

## Features

- *Order Management:*
  - Take customer orders efficiently.
  - View and manage order status.
  - Update orders as they progress through the system.

- *Menu Design:*
  - Easily design and update the restaurant menu.
  - Categorize items and set prices.
  - Add new items and remove outdated ones.

- *Food Preparation:*
  - Track the status of food preparation.
  - Assign tasks to kitchen staff.
  - Ensure timely and accurate preparation of dishes.
